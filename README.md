## 🧱 About Me

> **Time to think big again.**

From **financial advisor** to **software developer** — in 2025 I decided to stop selling products and start building them. Today I work fullstack: **Python, Django &amp; DRF** on the backend, **Angular** on the frontend — from the first model to the deployed container.

**Background:** German–Russian — one foot in both worlds, proud of both  
**Self:** Big nerd. A **Potterhead — by order of the Peaky Blinders.**

Outside of code you'll find me with a podcast in my ears, an anime on screen, or a controller in hand. But most of all: **music** — preferably live, preferably at a festival. 🎶
